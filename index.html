Afsah Anwar

Security Researcher

I am looking for research positions. If you have a vacancy where I can contribute, feel free to get in touch! Suggestions are appreciated too :)

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Khoury College of Computer Sciences at Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Alina Oprea. I obtained my doctorate degree in Computer Science from the Department of Computer Science at the University of Central Florida (UCF). At UCF, I was advised by David Mohaisen.

I enjoy building tools that help ease efforts at the different stages of incident response life cycle. My research efforts have resulted in tools and frameworks that impact the lives of incident responders.

Research Interests:

  • Threat Intelligence
  • Malware Analysis
  • Network Security
  • Security Measurements
  • Vulnerability Management
